A useful recipe tells you what to do. A memorable recipe tells you why it works, where it comes from, and how to make it your own without losing the point of the dish.

## Explain the Technique

Creators build trust when they explain heat, texture, timing, and substitutions. Viewers can follow a video once, but technique helps them cook with confidence later.

## Share the Origin

Context matters. A short note about the dish, the region, or the family habit behind it gives the recipe weight and helps people cook with more respect.

## Make the Next Step Obvious

End with serving ideas, storage notes, and one variation. That makes the recipe easier to return to and easier to recommend.
